RYOK?

Are you okay?



Based on information from the WHO in 2019, as many as 14% of adolescents in the world had mental illness. People with severe mental illness die an average of 10-20 years earlier than the general population.
In response to the health problems that are of concern to the world, especially in the field of mental health, we have created a website as a solution to these problems. We plan to educate more information about mental health and be a place for the RYOK? community to discuss and share with each other.
